Frequently Asked Questions

Do you service the porta-potties during long-term rentals?

Yes, our rentals include weekly cleaning, pumping, and restocking for ongoing sanitation.

Can I rent ADA-compliant portable toilets?

Absolutely. We offer ADA-accessible units for events and job sites requiring accessible restrooms.

What maintenance is included in long-term rentals?

Weekly cleaning, restocking, and waste pumping are included in long-term rental packages.

Can your restroom trailer rentals be used as temporary offices?

While our luxury portable restrooms are designed for sanitation, the high-end, climate-controlled interiors of our larger restroom trailer models have been used by clients as clean, private spaces for on-site managers in conjunction with their primary office trailers.

Does a restroom rental trailer require on-site staff?

For high-end galas and events, we highly recommend and can provide a uniformed attendant. The attendant keeps the portable restrooms for weddings or corporate events impeccably clean, restocks supplies, and ensures everything runs smoothly for your guests.

York Harbor, ME Insights: Population,
Zip Codes, Influence, and Service Areas

York Harbor is a census-designated place (CDP) in the town of York in York County, Maine, United States. The population was 3,033 at the 2010 census. York Harbor is a distinguished former Gilded Age summer colony noted for its resort architecture. It is part of the Portland–South Portland–Biddeford, Maine Metropolitan Statistical Area.
